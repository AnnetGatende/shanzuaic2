Voh Choir

VOH Choir - Voices of Harmony

The VOH Choir brings joy and inspiration to our congregation through music. We lead worship during services, perform at community events, and spread the message of hope and faith through song. Our dedicated choir members use their God-given talents to create an atmosphere where people can encounter God's presence.

Weekly Rehearsals

Join us every Wednesday and Saturday for practice sessions where we perfect our harmonies and learn new worship songs.

Community Outreach

We perform at weddings, community events, and outreach programs, spreading God's love through music.

Spiritual Growth

Beyond singing, we grow together in faith through prayer, fellowship, and studying God's Word.

Worship and Praise

Our choir members are dedicated musicians who uplift hearts and glorify God through harmonious worship. We lead the congregation in powerful praise that creates an atmosphere for encountering God's presence.

Community Engagement

We perform at church events, weddings, and community programs, using music to inspire hope and unity. Our outreach extends beyond the church walls to touch lives throughout our community.
