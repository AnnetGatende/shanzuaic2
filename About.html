About Us

AIC Shanzu CDC KE761

AIC Shanzu CDC is a Christ-centered, child-focused, church-driven organisation that exists to serve children and youth living in poverty. It is under Compassion International, which has been in existence for over 70 years. Compassion has been recognised for its efforts in child protection and safeguarding.

Our advocacy focuses on ensuring that each child is protected from abuse and exploitation ‚Äî every child must be known, loved and protected.

Activities

Through Compassion International's holistic child development program, we provide children with opportunities to develop spiritually, economically, socially and physically.

Our programs include educational support, health and nutrition services, life skills training, and spiritual nurturing. We work alongside local churches to ensure every child receives the care and support they need to break free from poverty and reach their full potential in Christ.
